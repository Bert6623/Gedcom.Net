My Family Tree


I just found the geocities webpage that I used to have in an archive website. So I have copied and pasted the information here and in the notes section on my facebook profile.

I have done more research and found more people, places and dates than what is on this list. I will update the information at some point when I have some free time (read when my grandchildren aren't here visiting me). In the meantime enjoy the information. What follows is what was posted on http://geocities.com/Heartland/Hills/6658/ahnentaf.html

This chart is possible because of the contributions of many people. Since I did not do all the research I can not guarantee that all the information contained here is 100% correct. If you find something that you believe is wrong please contact me. I do not want to be guilty of spreading wrong information. Of course it goes without saying that I also welcome hearing from you if you think we might have a connection! Send  a msg to me here or on facebook (http://www.facebook.com/#!/profile.php?id=100000337932283). This information was last updated on 23 August 2008.

Happy Hunting!
